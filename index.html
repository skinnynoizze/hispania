Our mission is to empower Latin ingenuity and drive global innovation.
Hispania Ventures is an independent early-stage private investment firm, founded in 2016. We focus on backing exceptional Latin talent in technology, media, health, and consumer industries worldwide. Our strategic investments and operational expertise help transform innovative ideas into market-leading companies.
With a deep understanding of both Latin American and global markets, we bridge cultural and business gaps to create unique opportunities. Our team's extensive experience and network enable us to provide not just capital, but also valuable guidance and connections to help our portfolio companies thrive on the international stage.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What types of startups does Hispania Ventures invest in?
Hispania Ventures focuses on early-stage startups in Latin America or founded by Latin American entrepreneurs. We primarily invest in technology, media, health, and consumer industries. We look for innovative companies with high growth potential and a vision to expand globally.
How does Hispania Ventures support its portfolio companies?
Beyond capital, we provide strategic guidance, operational expertise, and access to our global network. We help our portfolio companies navigate regional challenges, refine their business models, and connect with potential partners and customers worldwide. Our team's experience in both Latin American and global markets allows us to offer unique insights and opportunities.
What is Hispania Ventures' investment process?
Our investment process typically involves initial screening, due diligence, and partnership discussions. We start with a review of your pitch deck and financial projections. If there's a potential fit, we'll schedule meetings to understand your team, product, and market better. We aim to make the process as transparent and efficient as possible, usually completing it within 2-3 months from initial contact to investment decision.
